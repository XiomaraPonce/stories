I smile faintly as I remember I had made plans with someone this afternoon. Apparently arriving on the set time isn’t very common but I try to do so anyways even if they sometimes arrive later. Thankfully they didn’t today, not like it’d be much of an issue if they did.

Oh it’s a movie so maybe it would be.

We didn’t talk much, only a little before and after the movie. It was a fun experience though. Cinemark always plays those ads that seem to beg for you not to watch stuff on streaming, that “movies belong on the big screen” and whatnot.

They were right this time though. It was a story about being haunted by “what ifs”, constantly trying to outdo the you of yesterday, a thrilling sports drama where each character constantly fought for your attention.

Anime horse girls do deserve more than just a phone screen.

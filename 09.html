Campus looks different at night, though that could be said about any location really. Even with the slightly unsettling feeling in my stomach I take solace in the fact that I won’t be outside for long. Soon enough I find myself in the building for my night class, and after some trial and error I find myself in the classroom for my night class.

I mull over the meaning of life. Why do people say it’s 42? Who decided that? Why am I thinking of such a thing at this time? Probably because it’s late and my mind is running on fumes. I’ve gotten better about needing less hours of sleep but I still need more than the average person my age.

Is it already time?
